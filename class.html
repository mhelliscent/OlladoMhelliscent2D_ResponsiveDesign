Body Building

Building your body makes you more healthy and you can also enjoy socializing with other work out buddy on our Fitness Hub. Bodybuilding has such great and healthy impact on muscle, bones and joints. Bodybuilding keeps your body and muscle strong and flexible. Bodybuilding and weight training can definitely help you with osteoporosis and arthritis. These health benefits of weight training and aerobic exercise are already well known.

Muscle Building

Muscle building will help you shape your muscle just like you wanted with the help of our trainors. The key to building muscle is to increase the rate of protein deposition while minimizing the rate of protein breakdown. This process of increasing your muscle mass is known as muscle hypertrophy, and it's a primary goal of resistance training.

Body Mass Index

What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a person's weight in kilograms (or pounds) divided by the square of height in meters (or feet). A high BMI can indicate high body fatness.
